Introduction

2020-06-26 :: saso

Hello!

Having finally gotten this site up and running, I thought it would be time to do an introductory post about who I am and what I’ll be publishing here.

Information security is a lifestyle for me as well as a profession. Outside of work, I’m usually doing CTFs, researching new methodologies and attack types, and working on a certification or course of some kind.

I want this to be a place where I share things I’ve learned in different forms. You can expect write-ups of CTF challenges, reviews of certifications, guides on lesser-known security topics, and more.

I’m a privacy advocate and love a good FLOSS project, so I’ll be talking about the ones I use from time to time as well as publishing my own.

Lastly, I work as a penetration tester, so anything I find or develop that I think can improve te industry (and therefore security as a whole) will end up here.
